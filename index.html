About Me

I am a PhD Research Scholar in Zoology at JAIN (Deemed-to-be) University, Bengaluru, affiliated with the Centre for Urban Ecology, Biodiversity, Evolution & Climate Change (CUBEC).

My research examines how rapid urbanisation and urban heat island effects drive morphological, locomotor, and physiological adaptations in reptiles, using geckos as a model system to understand urban evolution and climate-driven selection.

I am a funded early-career researcher, supported by competitive international grants, and actively engaged in national and international scientific conferences.
