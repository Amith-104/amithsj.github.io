About Me

I am a PhD Research Scholar in Zoology at JAIN (Deemed-to-be) University, Bengaluru, working at the Centre for Urban Ecology, Biodiversity, Evolution & Climate Change (CUBEC).

My research examines how rapid urbanisation and urban heat island effects shape morphology, locomotor performance, and physiological tolerance in reptiles, using geckos as a model system.
